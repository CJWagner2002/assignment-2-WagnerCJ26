Software Engineering at NDSU

Hello my name is Christopher Wagner and you can call me a little bit of an expert about my time here in Fargo, ND; saying Ive been here my whole life and all. Growing up here has been interesting to say the least, going though all the seasons and not really traveling that much outside of the general area. Well after graduating from North High School in Fargo, I ended up going to NDSU for my major. Here I started off going as an electrical engineer and even found off with a new friend. Well as that friendship continued things started taking a turn and left me in a depressing state. I eventually got over it and decided to switch my major to software engineering. Becasue of that change and the major not yet available at NDSU, I breafly switched it over to computer science to get some of the other credits needed for that major done. Because of that I am on track to graduate NDSU next spring semester being one of the first people to graduate NDSU with that said degree! All I hope is that I get a good well rounded intership this semester so I can continue with my career and move into an appartment.
